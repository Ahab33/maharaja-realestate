Our Story

Founded in 2001, Maharaja Builders & Realtors began with a simple vision: to redefine luxury living in India. What started as a small family business has grown into one of the most trusted names in premium real estate.

Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of over 10,000 families who call our properties home.

Today, we continue to set new standards in the industry, combining traditional values with modern innovation to create properties that truly stand the test of time.
